- Max 5-7 words per caption

## App Icon

### Specifications

| Platform | Sizes Needed |
|----------|--------------|
| iOS | 1024 Ã— 1024 (App Store), auto-scaled |
| Android | 512 Ã— 512 (Play Store), adaptive icon |

### Design Direction

**Option A: Footprint**
- Baby footprint icon
- Soft, rounded design
- Pastel teal background

**Option B: Journey Path**
- Curved dotted path
- Small milestone markers
- Represents "journey"

**Option C: Heart + Baby**
- Minimalist heart shape
- Small baby silhouette
- Emotional, universal

**Recommended: Option A (Footprint)**
- Instantly recognizable as baby-related
- Works at small sizes
- Unique among competitor icons

### Icon Requirements

- No text in icon (won't be readable at small sizes)
- No photos (will look muddy)
- Simple, 2-3 colors maximum
- Test at 29pt (smallest iOS display size)
